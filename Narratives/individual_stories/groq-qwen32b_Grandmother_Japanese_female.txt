**Title: Hana and the Whispering Willow**  

Once, in a quiet village nestled beneath ancient mountains, lived a curious girl named Hana. Every evening, she wandered the forest near her grandmother’s home, where a willow tree stood, its branches shimmering like silver threads. One twilight, as fireflies danced, Hana heard a soft sob. Following the sound, she found a small, translucent spirit, its form swirling like mist.  

“Are you lost, *mushi*?” Hana asked gently. The spirit nodded, revealing it was a *yurei*, a forest spirit, unable to cross a river blocking its path.
